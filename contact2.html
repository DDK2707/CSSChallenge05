Disturbed Discography

Evolution (2018)

The latest album, boasting a lighter but no less powerful sound

Live at Red Rocks (2016)

A collection of Disturbed's most famous songs, performed at Red Rocks Ampitheatre

Immortalized (2015)

Featuring the title track "Immortalized", as well as a cover of Simon and Garfunkel's "The Sound of Silence"

The Lost Children (2011)

A collection of B-side songs, including a cover of "Living After Midnight" by Judas Priest

Asylum (2010)

More mature themes mark the band's evolution (hehe), as stated by lead singer David Draiman

Indestructible (2008)

The first self-produced album, produced by lead guitarist Dan Donegan

Ten Thousand Fists (2005)

The second consecutive number 1 release on the Billboard 200, featuring title song "Ten Thousand Fists"

Believe (2002)

Songs on "Believe" feature religious and spiritual themes as a result of contemporary events, to compliment the album cover's combination of different religious symbols, meant to represent unity

The Sickness (2000)

The band's debut album, with insanely popular song "Down with the Sickness", the band's first single to be certified platinum. The album itself has been certified five times platinum and is the band's most successful album
