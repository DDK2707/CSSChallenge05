Linkin Park Discography

One More Light (2017)

The latest album, and the last with lead singer Chester Bennington before his passing two months after release

The Hunting Party (2014)

A departure from the electronic rock of the last two albums, returning to the nu metal sound of Hybrid Theory

Recharged (2013)

Remixes of songs from the album "Living Things", with a feature by Steve Aoki on the first track, "A Light That Never Comes"

Living Things (2012)

A merging of elements from the previous four albums, remaining in now comfortable territory for the band, while still having lyrical content that speaks to personal issues faced by the band's members

A Thousand Suns (2010)

A concept album departing from previous sounds. Dealing with human fears, Shinoda states that the album references various social issues and the blend of human and technology

Minutes to Midnight (2007)

A melding of punk, classic rock and hip-hop, with Mike Shinoda stepping up to sing in many of the songs

Meteora (2003)

A similar sound to the debut album, "Meteora" features the incredibly popular songs "Faint", "Numb", and "Breaking the Habit"

Reanimation (2002)

A remix album of songs from "Hybrid Theory", produced by Shinoda. It is the fifth best selling remix album of all time

Hybrid Theory (2000)

The debut album, named after the band's previous name. The lyrics deal with challenges that Bennington faced in his adolescence
